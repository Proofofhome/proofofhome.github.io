# About ProofOfHome

## Why ProofOfHome Exists

Modern life depends on increasingly complex systems.

Our money, technology, data, communication, and energy are managed through systems that most people use every day but rarely understand.

These systems provide enormous benefits, but dependence without understanding creates vulnerability.

ProofOfHome started from a simple question:

**Can an ordinary household become more resilient by understanding and controlling more of the technology it depends on?**

This is not about disconnecting from society or rejecting modern technology.

It is about becoming more capable within it.

By learning how systems work, repairing instead of replacing, using open technology, and building alternatives where practical, individuals can regain more control over the tools that shape their lives.

---

## What is ProofOfHome?

ProofOfHome is about practical resilience — reducing unnecessary dependence on systems we don't control by learning how technology works and building alternatives where they make sense.

The project explores how individuals can build more sovereign, private, and resilient homes using open technology.

By experimenting with Bitcoin, self-hosted infrastructure, hardware projects, energy systems, and privacy-focused tools, ProofOfHome aims to make complex technology more accessible and understandable.

---

## Vision

A future where ordinary households understand and control more of the systems they rely on.

A sovereign home is not a home disconnected from the world.

It is a home that:

- understands its technology
- controls its data
- verifies its money
- uses open and repairable systems
- builds resilience through knowledge

---

## Mission

Build, test, document, and share real-world experiments involving:

- Bitcoin infrastructure
- Self-hosted systems
- Hardware projects
- Privacy and security
- Home technology
- Energy systems
- Emerging technologies

Every experiment will document:

- What was built
- Why it was tested
- Hardware and costs
- Challenges encountered
- Lessons learned
