# Roadmap

I'm thinking about this in seasons. Nothing here is fixed — the plan is to only move to the next one once the current one actually has real results behind it, not just a write-up of what I intend to do.

## Season 1 — Bitcoin Foundation (in progress)

Get a Bitcoin node actually running at home, on hardware I already had lying around. Document the setup, what it cost (mostly nothing so far), and how long it actually took to sync.

## Season 2 — Sovereign Home Server

Once the node's sorted, I want to start self-hosting more of the stuff I currently just hand to someone else's cloud — files, backups, a dashboard to keep an eye on all of it.

## Season 3 — Physical Builds

Some hands-on projects — small electronics, sensors, maybe a Bitcoin display of some kind. More soldering iron, less keyboard.

## Season 4 — Energy Independence

Solar, batteries, backup power. I want real numbers here, not marketing numbers — how much it actually costs, how much it actually saves, what breaks.

## Season 5 — Practical Resilience

The unglamorous stuff: backup power for the essentials, water storage, basic food storage, off-grid comms. Not prepping for the end of the world — just less fragile if something ordinary goes wrong for a while.

That's the rough shape of it. Ask me again in six months and it'll probably have shifted.
